Weiis-Aug Cp. Inc. starts operations in Mexico


Weiss-Aug Co. Inc. a North American company headquartered in East Hanover, NJ began operations in a custom-built 2,638 square meter building at the Airport Industrial Park in Apodaca, Nuevo Leon. This building will be the first manufacturing operation outside of the United States for Weiss-Aug Co.


Weiss manufacturing facility is part of Fibra NOVA portfolio.


Weiss-Aug was founded in 1972 providing custom insert molding, precision medical and automotive metal stamping and assembly solutions.
