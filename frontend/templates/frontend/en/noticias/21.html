Visteon celebrates opening ceremony of new technical center in Queretaro


Matthew Cole, Visteon Senior Vice President of Product Development; Liseth Reyes, Visteon Site Leader; Marco Antonio del Prete, Secretary of Development of the State of Queretaro; Francisco Dominguez Servin, Governor of the State of Queretaro; Francisco Vazquez, Visteon Project Leader, among other dignitaries, celebrated the inauguration ceremony of Visteon's new 22,770 square meter facility that includes 6,000 square meters of office space on two floors and 450 parking spaces.


Visteon Corporation is a US public company a leader of the digital revolution in automotive electronic. Visteon starts this operation with 450 engineers developing software for intelligent and digital cockpit electronics products for North America and will reach 600 engineers by 2020.
