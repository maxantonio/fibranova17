Fibra Nova signs a purchase and sale agreement


Fibra Nova signed a binding agreement with a promise to purchase for a 192 thousand square foot industrial building located in Cd Juarez leased to the transnational Electrolux, due diligence will be completed by the end of the first quarter at the latest.


With this transaction, the annualized dollar revenues of our portfolio will increase from 42% to 45%. During the first 45 days of the year 2021, the new investments we have announced reached US$33 million.
